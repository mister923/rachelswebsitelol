About Me

I'm Rachelle Goldberg, an artist passionate about exploring the intersection of organic forms and geometric precision. My work reflects the harmony between natural beauty and structured design, creating pieces that speak to both the heart and the mind.

Through my art, I seek to capture the essence of transformation and growth, using a blend of traditional techniques and contemporary approaches. Each piece is a journey of discovery, where intuition meets intention.
